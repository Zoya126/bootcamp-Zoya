The Kite Runner

by Khaled Hosseini

Genre: Drama, Historical Fiction

The Kite Runner is a novel about the story of Amir, a young boy from Kabul, and his complicated relationship with his friend Hassan, set against the backdrop of Afghanistan's political upheaval.

One Indian Girl

by Chetan Bhagat

Genre: Fiction, Romance

One Indian Girl is a novel about Radhika Mehta, a successful career woman, and her struggles with societal expectations, love, and relationships.

The Alchemist

by Paulo Coelho

Genre: Adventure, Philosophy

The Alchemist tells the story of Santiago, a shepherd boy who embarks on a journey to find a hidden treasure and discovers that the true treasure lies within himself.

The Sun and Her Flowers

by Rupi Kaur

Genre: Poetry

The Sun and Her Flowers is a collection of poems by Rupi Kaur that explores themes of love, loss, trauma, healing, and self-discovery.

The Power of Your Subconscious Mind

by Joseph Murphy

Genre: Self-help, Psychology

The Power of Your Subconscious Mind explores the potential of the subconscious mind and how its power can be harnessed for personal transformation and success.
