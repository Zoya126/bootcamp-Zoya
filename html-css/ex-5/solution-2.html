Cool Places Around the World

Santorini, Greece

Fun Fact: Santorini's volcano erupted around 3,600 years ago, and it’s believed to have inspired the Atlantis myth!

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

Fun Fact: The Great Barrier Reef is so large it can be seen from space, stretching over 2,300 kilometers!

Machu Picchu, Peru

Fun Fact: Machu Picchu was built in the 15th century by the Inca Empire, and was rediscovered by Hiram Bingham in 1911.

Dubai, UAE

Fun Fact: Dubai is home to the world’s tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, which stands at a staggering 828 meters tall!

Tokyo, Japan

Fun Fact: Tokyo is home to the world’s busiest train station, Shinjuku Station, with over 3.5 million passengers daily!
